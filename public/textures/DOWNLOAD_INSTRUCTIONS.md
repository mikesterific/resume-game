# Professional Texture Downloads
*Step-by-step instructions for getting high-quality materials*

## 🎯 **Download Links & Instructions**

### **1. Floor Textures (Wood)**
**Direct Link**: https://ambientcg.com/view?id=WoodFloor040

**Steps**:
1. Click the link above
2. Scroll down to "Download" section
3. Select **1K-JPG** (recommended for performance)
4. Download these 4 files:
   - `WoodFloor040_1K-JPG_Color.jpg` → Rename to `floor/diffuse.jpg`
   - `WoodFloor040_1K-JPG_NormalGL.jpg` → Rename to `floor/normal.jpg`
   - `WoodFloor040_1K-JPG_Roughness.jpg` → Rename to `floor/roughness.jpg`
   - `WoodFloor040_1K-JPG_AmbientOcclusion.jpg` → Rename to `floor/ao.jpg`

## 📁 **File Placement**
After downloading, your structure should be:

```
public/textures/
├── floor/
│   ├── diffuse.jpg     (Wood color)
│   ├── normal.jpg      (Wood bumps)
│   ├── roughness.jpg   (Wood surface)
│   └── ao.jpg          (Wood shadows)
├── ceiling/
│   ├── diffuse.jpg     (Ceiling color)
│   ├── normal.jpg      (Ceiling details)
│   └── roughness.jpg   (Ceiling surface)
└── walls/
    ├── diffuse.jpg     (Wall color)
    ├── normal.jpg      (Wall texture)
    ├── roughness.jpg   (Wall surface)
    └── displacement.jpg (Wall depth)
```
